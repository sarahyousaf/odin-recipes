Creamy Garlic Pasta Recipe

Description

This recipe details how to make a stunning Creamy Garlic Pasta. This recipe only takes 25 minutes in total to prepare and cook therefore making it the perfect choice for a midweek meal. This pasta is also nut free and contains minimal levels of sugar making it the perfect choice for the family.

Ingredients

  • 300g tagliatelle
  • 20g salted butter
  • 6 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 150ml chicken stock
  • 200ml double cream
  • 85g grated parmesan
  • 20g flat-leaf parsely, roughly chopped

Method

  1. Cook the pasta and melt the butter in a seperate pan. Once the butter is foaming, add in the garlic and let cook for 4-5 minutes
  2. Stir in the flour to form a paste. Stir for 1-2 mins, then slowly whisk in the chicken stock until you have a smooth sauce. Pour in the double cream and sprinkle in most of the parmesan. Stir until everything is melted and combined, then season to taste.
  3. Transfer the pasta straight into the sauce, alongside a good splash of the cooking water. Mix thoroughly
  4. Serve with a topping of chopped parsely and the remaining parmesan
